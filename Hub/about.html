About Pegasus Suite LLC

The company behind PropArt™ and the Craft In Ardor suite of tools for makers.

Mission

Pegasus Suite LLC builds focused software for serious hobbyists and small creative businesses—especially in the polymer clay and handmade worlds—so you can plan work, protect your originals, and share what you make without fighting the internet. We believe artists deserve fair recognition and compensation, and that tools should feel calm and human, not like a corporate maze.

Vision

A PropArt™ ecosystem where each app does one job brilliantly, while your account and habits carry quietly across the suite—so you spend more time making and less time managing tabs, exports, and logins.
