### SaaS Valuation Formula
$$\text{Valuation} = \text{ARR} \times \text{Multiplier}$$

Where:
- **ARR** = Annual Recurring Revenue (monthly × 12)
- **Multiplier** = 4-8x (based on growth & quality)

**Example:** $1.8M ARR × 5x = $9M valuation
