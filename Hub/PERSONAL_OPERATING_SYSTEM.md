# Personal Operating System (10-Year Edition)

Use this page as your default reset. Revisit monthly.

## 1) Direction

**10-year intent (who I am becoming):**
- A healthy, calm, financially secure creator with meaningful work and strong relationships.

**This year's theme (single sentence):**
- Build durable systems that protect energy and grow income without chaos.

## 2) Non-Negotiables

- Sleep: 7+ hours average.
- Body: Move daily (walk, stretch, train, or dance).
- Mind: 10 minutes of quiet (prayer, journaling, breathing, meditation).
- Relationships: One intentional check-in with someone I care about each week.
- Money: Auto-invest on schedule; no skipped contributions.

## 4) Decision Rules

Use these to reduce overthinking:

- If it harms health or peace, it is too expensive.
- If it does not support this year's theme, it is a "not now."
- If the task can be automated, templatized, or delegated, do that first.
- If I am emotionally flooded, wait 24 hours before major decisions.
- Choose the option future-me will thank me for in 3 years.

## 9) Anti-Burnout Triggers

- Poor sleep 3+ nights
- Irritability/high anxiety
- Avoiding important work
- Doomscrolling spike
- No joy all week

Reset protocol:
1. Sleep + hydration + walk.
2. Cancel/reschedule low-priority items.
3. Return to one simple "Daily Top 1."
